About Us

Who we are, our founding, and more

 

Our Founders

The nonprofit organization of Enviroco was founded on January 8th, 2024 by college students Ella Stewards and Lindsay Morgan.

Together, they desire to improve the conditions on our planet.

   

Our Mission

The Earth has been decaying for many years now; it's a common fact.

Our mission is to retore the beauty of our planet via natural and sustainable methods

We strive to bring down carbon per part rates, decrease the usage of non-renewable energy, and ensure clean water and land for our animals.

So far, we've raised almost $3,000 charity and cleaned up over 20 locations across Florida.

We're always working towards ensuring a better future for everyone and evertything on Earth.
